In an extraordinary display of community solidarity, Ramadan Giving's annual gala raised over $197,000 for families affected by the humanitarian crisis in Gaza. This record-breaking event brought together hundreds of supporters united by a common purpose: to help those in desperate need.

A Night of Generosity

The gala, held at the Toronto Convention Centre, welcomed over 400 guests from across the GTA. The evening featured inspiring speakers, including families who have benefited from our programs and volunteers who have dedicated countless hours to our cause.

Where the Funds Are Going

Every dollar raised is being directed to provide immediate relief to families in Gaza, including:

  • Emergency Food Supplies: Providing nutritious meals to families facing food insecurity
  • Medical Aid: Supporting healthcare facilities and medical supply distribution
  • Shelter Support: Helping displaced families find temporary housing
  • Children's Programs: Ensuring children have access to education and psychological support

Community Partners

We extend our heartfelt gratitude to our sponsors and community partners who made this event possible. Their generosity and commitment to humanitarian causes continue to inspire our work.

Looking Forward

While we celebrate this achievement, we know there is still much work to be done. The crisis in Gaza continues, and families need our ongoing support. We remain committed to providing relief and hope to those who need it most.

Thank you to everyone who attended, donated, and spread the word. Together, we are making a difference.
