2025 marked an extraordinary milestone for Ramadan Giving. Through the unwavering dedication of our volunteers and the generous support of our community, we achieved record-breaking numbers across all our programs.

GTA Highlights

In the Greater Toronto Area, our teams distributed 1,965 lunch bags to those experiencing food insecurity, 530 winter kits to help families stay warm during the cold months, and 460 food packages to households in need.

We also served over 430 hot meals to unhoused community members and distributed 150+ toy bags to bring joy to children during the holiday season.

"The spirit of giving knows no bounds. Every lunch bag, every winter kit, every smile we bring represents a connection—a reminder that we are all part of one community." — Ramadan Giving Team

Children's Camp Day

Our annual camp day brought together 111 children for a day filled with activities, games, and memorable experiences. It's moments like these that remind us why we do what we do.

Gaza Relief Efforts

In response to the humanitarian crisis in Gaza, our community came together like never before, raising over $108,000 for relief efforts. These funds are providing essential supplies, medical aid, and support to families affected by the conflict.

Egypt Operations

Our Cairo team distributed 370 food packs and 330 meat packs to vulnerable families. We also hosted a camp day for 63 children, including Gazan evacuees who found temporary refuge in Egypt.

Looking Forward

As we reflect on this incredible year, we're filled with gratitude for every volunteer, donor, and community member who made this possible. Together, we're not just providing aid—we're building bridges of hope that connect communities across continents.

Thank you for being part of our journey. Here's to an even more impactful 2026!
