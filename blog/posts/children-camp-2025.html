This year's camp day was our most impactful yet, bringing joy and celebration to 174 children across two continents. In the GTA, 111 children enjoyed a day of activities, while in Cairo, 63 children—including Gazan evacuees—experienced the magic of community and belonging.

A Day of Joy in the GTA

Our Toronto camp day transformed a local community center into a wonderland of activities. Children participated in arts and crafts, sports, games, and special performances. The highlight of the day was the closing ceremony where each child received a gift bag filled with toys and treats.

"I've never seen my son smile so much in one day. Thank you for giving him this experience." — A grateful parent

Cairo: A Special Mission

This year's Cairo camp held special significance. Among the 63 children we served were families who had evacuated from Gaza and found temporary refuge in Egypt. For these children, the camp offered a rare day of normalcy amid extraordinary circumstances.

Our Cairo team, in partnership with local organizations, provided:

  • Full meals and refreshments throughout the day
  • Educational activities and games
  • Psychological support activities designed for children who have experienced trauma
  • Gift bags with school supplies, toys, and clothing

The Power of Play

Research shows that play is essential for children's development and well-being, especially during times of stress. Our camp days are designed to provide not just entertainment, but meaningful experiences that support emotional health and build lasting memories.

Volunteer Heroes

None of this would be possible without our incredible volunteers. Over 50 volunteers across both locations dedicated their time and energy to making this day special. From face painting to organizing games, their enthusiasm was contagious.

Every child deserves to feel joy. Thank you for helping us deliver it.
