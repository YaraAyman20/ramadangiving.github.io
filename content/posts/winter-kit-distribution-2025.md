### What's in a Winter Kit?

Each kit is carefully assembled to provide essential warmth:

| Item | Quantity | Purpose |
|------|----------|---------|
| Winter jacket | 1 per person | Primary warmth |
| Thermal underlayers | 2 sets | Layering protection |

| Winter hat | 1 | Head warmth |
| Scarf | 1 | Neck protection |
| Hand warmers | 10 packs | Emergency warmth |

**Total value per kit: Approximately $200**

### The Distribution Process

Getting winter kits to those who need them most requires careful planning:

**Phase 1: Needs Assessment**
- Partner with social workers and community organizations
- Identify families most in need
- Gather sizing information for personalized kits

**Phase 2: Assembly**
- Source quality items at discounted rates
- Organize packing events with volunteers
- Customize kits based on family composition

**Phase 3: Distribution**
- Schedule pick-up appointments
- Arrange delivery for those who can't travel
- Provide extra support as needed
